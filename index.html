Past, Present and Future

The use of tools has been so critical to the development of the human species, as building tools gives us greater capabilities to build more tools.

Past Present Future

Believe it or not the use of tools have been estimated to predate Homo sapiens by 100,000 years. Even now Homo sapiens are not the only species to use tools, with our primate cousins the most notably being the Capuchin Monkey that uses rocks to crack nuts.
